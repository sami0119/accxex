Changing Perceptions
“The investor in my head was someone who wore a suit and a tie. Accxex changed that for me.”
Maria, 26
Growing up, Maria’s family didn’t talk about money—so when she wanted to start investing, she had no idea where to begin. Enter: Accxex. By investing just a small amount to start, she was able to learn at her own pace, on her own terms. After three years, she was able to make progress on some big financial goals, including paying off her student debt.
Small steps to big dreams
“I’m able to make financial decisions that grow my money.”
Alex, 34
Investing has always been available to those who’ve achieved their financial goals. But what about those who haven’t? For Alex, his dream of buying a house always felt out of reach. With Accxex, he was able to start working towards this big goal through small, daily investments.
Empowering people
“In a male-dominated world, it’s really important for women to invest. I feel good when I can chime in—it gives me a confidence boost.”
Laura, 22
The benefits of investing are universal—no matter your gender. And for women, minorities, and young people, the ability to start slowly at your own pace has been a breakthrough. For Laura, information and education resources on Accxex have empowered her to add investing to her financial toolkit.
Forming habits
“I’m making learning a habit. I feel way more confident about the choices I make.”
Jade, 25
Jade’s plan is based around longevity, and how to build a future where he’s not worrying about money. He set short-term goals to make sense of how the markets work—and was able to fulfill them by listening to Accxex Snacks each morning. By bringing investing news into his day-to-day, Jade’s been able to make sense of the markets in his own way.
